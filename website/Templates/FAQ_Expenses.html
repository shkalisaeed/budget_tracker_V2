Expenses FAQs & Documentation

Expenses can be uploaded in bulk using CSVs provided by your bank or added individually. To add expenses individually, click on the "Add Expense" button on the Pofile page. You will be prompted to enter the expense name, amount, and date. You can also add a description and category for the expense. Once you have entered all the information, click "Add Expense" to add the expense to your budget. Bulk expenses can be added by following the tutorial on the Upload CSV page in the profile section.
Yes, you can. When you're creating a new expense entry in The Budget Manager, you have the option to categorize it. This helps you keep track of your spending in different areas and can provide valuable insights into your spending habits. If necessary, you can also edit the category of an expense later on.
Recurring expenses can be set up as Bills, which are explained in the Bills FAQs section.
