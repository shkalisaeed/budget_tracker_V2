Budget Creation FAQs & Documentation

To create a budget in The Budget Manager, you need to select "Budget Creator" from the top panel of the interface. After clicking on this, you'll be presented with an "add new" option. Click on this and proceed to fill in the details of your new budget, including the budget's name, amount, and duration. Once you have input all the necessary information, you can save the budget and it will be added to your account.
