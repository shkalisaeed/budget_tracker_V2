Dashboard FAQs & Documentation

The current version of The Budget Manager offers a predefined dashboard layout. This design has been chosen to present a broad overview of the most pertinent financial information. However, the developers understand the importance of a customizable user experience and are actively working on an update that will allow users to customize their dashboards to better fit their individual needs.
The dashboard on The Budget Manager is designed to give you a high-level overview of your financial status at a glance. For more detailed reports or transaction history, you would need to navigate to the specific sections via the top panel. Each section will provide a more granular view of your financial data, including transaction histories and detailed reports on your budget, expenses, income, and investments.
The dashboard on The Budget Manager is dynamic and updates in real-time. This means that every time you add a new entry, whether it's an expense, income, or a change in your budget or goals, the dashboard immediately reflects these changes. This ensures you always have the most accurate and up-to-date information on your financial status.
