Goals FAQs & Documentation

Setting financial goals in The Budget Manager is easy. You can set your financial goals in the "Goals" section by clicking on the "Add New" button. This allows you to define what you are aiming for and track your progress over time.
Certainly. The Budget Manager allows you to track your progress towards your financial goals in the "Goals" section. If you need to make any adjustments to a goal, you can do so by clicking on the "Edit" button next to the goal in question. This feature ensures that your goals stay relevant and aligned with your current financial situation.
