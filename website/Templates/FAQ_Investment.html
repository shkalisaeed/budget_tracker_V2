Investments FAQs & Documentation

Absolutely. Currently, The Budget Manager supports the addition of a range of investment types including cryptocurrencies, stocks, and bonds. This way, you can keep track of all your investments in one place. We're always working to enhance the functionality of our application, and future updates will include the capability to add even more types of investments.
The Budget Manager offers real-time updates on your investment portfolio. This means that whenever you log into the application, you can see the current value of your investments based on the latest market data. This allows you to monitor your investments more effectively and make informed financial decisions.
The Budget Manager does not offer analysis reports for your investments. However, it does provide the data nessesary to do so.
